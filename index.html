About the Visualization ➡

About the Visualization

Detailed Essay
  • Messaging:
    • Chicago crime trends are on the rise in 2024 for both domestic and non-domestic incidents.
    • Despite non-domestic crimes being four times more frequent compared to domestic crimes, the arrest percentage is higher for domestic crimes.
  • Narrative Structure: MARTINI GLASS HYBRID STRUCTURE
    • Share author observed insights with readers using a streamlined message-focused, non-interactive scenes 1 & 2.
    • Follow with an interactive scene (scene 3) which will enable users to explore these observations further.
  • Scenes:
    • Scene 1: Non-Domestic Crime Trends (Non-Interactive).
    • Scene 2: Domestic Crime Trends (Non-Interactive).
    • Scene 3: Arrest rates by Domestic & Non-Domestic crimes - (Interactive | use Tooltips & radio buttons)
  • Annotations:
    • A red dashed rectangle to highlight the range of data points central to the messaging.
    • A text providing context is colored to match the red rectangle, creating a visual association.
  • Parameters:
    • Scene 1: Crime type | State = Non-domestic | Function =renderChart().
    • Scene 2: Crime type | State = Domestic | Function =renderChart().
    • Scene 3: Crime type | State = Domestic,Non-domestic, both .
  • Triggers:
    • Scene 1,2: Animation will be triggered automatically when the scene / slide is displayed.
    • Scene 3: Selection of Radio button change states (Domestic, Non-Domestic or both) triggers the chart to show the line chart corresponding to the parameter selected.
  • Transition:
    • Scene 1,2,3: Animation will be triggered after the scene is selected
    • Scene 1,2: Annotations will appear after the transition on line chart is completed.
  • Accordance:
    • Scene 3: *Information Marker* is added to the scene to cue the reader to hover over for tooltip.
To Scene 1 ➡

Non-Domestic Crimes: Worrying Upward Trend in 2024 After Decline in 2023

Non-domestic crimes in Chicago saw a dramatic peak in July 2023, followed by a sharp decrease that hit its lowest point in January 2024. The latest data reveals a worrying upward trend, signaling the need for heightened vigilance and caution.

To Scene 2 ➡

Domestic Crime : Stability Gives Way to Upward Trend in 2024

Chicago's domestic crime rates peaked in July 2023, declined to a low in November 2023, stabilized early in 2024, but began rising again in April, nearly reaching previous summer levels by May.

To Scene 3 ➡

Arrest Rates Higher in Domestic Cases Despite Lower Crime Incidents!

In a striking revelation, domestic arrest % surpass those of non-domestic arrests, with a notable convergence in January 2024. The data reveals that the average number of domestic crime incidents over the year hovers around 18,000, while non-domestic crimes average below 4,000 annually. **Use tooltips and radio buttons for interactivity**.
